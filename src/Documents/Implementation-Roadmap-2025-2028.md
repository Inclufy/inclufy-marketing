### Technical Risks
1. **AI Model Performance**
   - Mitigation: Multiple model providers, own model training
   
2. **Scalability**
   - Mitigation: Early investment in infrastructure

3. **Data Privacy**
   - Mitigation: Privacy-by-design, federated learning

### Business Risks
1. **Competition from Big Tech**
   - Mitigation: Deep customer moat, faster innovation
   
2. **Market Education**
   - Mitigation: Strong content marketing, case studies

3. **Talent Acquisition**
   - Mitigation: Remote-first, equity participation
