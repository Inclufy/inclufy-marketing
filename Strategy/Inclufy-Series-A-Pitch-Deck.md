- **Minute 3**: Live demo wow
- **Minute 7**: Traction reveal  
- **Minute 12**: Vision unlock
- **Minute 18**: Clear ask

### Likely Questions & Answers

**Q: "What if OpenAI/Google builds this?"**
A: "They build horizontal AI. We own the vertical - deep marketing expertise, customer data, and workflow integration they'll never have."

**Q: "How defensible is this really?"**
A: "Every day, our AI makes 1M decisions and learns from outcomes. A competitor starting today would need 3 years to catch up, and we're not standing still."

**Q: "What's your enterprise strategy?"**
A: "We're already landing Fortune 500s. Enterprise features in Q2 2026, dedicated team in place, 7-figure ACVs by year 3."

**Q: "Why won't customers just use ChatGPT?"**
A: "Show me ChatGPT running a multi-channel campaign, making real-time decisions, and improving from results. That's the difference between a tool and a platform."

### Closing Statement
"The question isn't whether marketing will become autonomous. It's who will own the platform that makes it possible. With your partnership, that's Inclufy."
